Welcome to EPCC's ACM Computer Science Club page.
Becoming a member of our club will help expand your knowledge and improve your programming skills. There will be a series of workshops throughout the school year to share knowledge on topics such as: building your own PC, learning new programming languages, and more. Joining the club will also give you the opportunity to interact with more students in the program and learn from their experiences and knowledge.

Hackers Corner

Come join us every Friday at 6:00 PM at Starbucks located in The Fountains at Farah. During this fun meeting, students meet up with their fellow classmates as well as tutors to drink coffee, work on assignments and socialize. Hackers Corner is a fun, educational, and optional meeting for our fellow Computer Science peers.
